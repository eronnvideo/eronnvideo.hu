I am Aron, a wedding filmmaker.

I create wedding films that carry the atmosphere of the day, not just the sequence of events. I believe a good filmmaker does not take over the moment, but quietly stays present for it.

I observe, wait, and capture real gestures: a look, a laugh, a movement that would otherwise pass unnoticed. I keep the process calm, so you can stay inside the day rather than perform for the camera.

My style is clean, cinematic and natural. I work with multiple cameras so the key moments remain complete from more than one perspective, while drone footage adds a quiet sense of scale.

I am not looking for staged scenes. I am looking for your story, as it really felt. If you want a film you will still want to watch ten years from now, you are in the right place.
